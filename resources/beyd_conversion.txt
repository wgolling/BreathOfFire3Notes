The weapon/armor that you give to Beyd during his combat with Zig will be upgraded.
The equipment you receive will have IDs one larger than the ones you give him.
For example, giving him Scramasax and Scale Mail gives Moon Sword and Crepe Cape.

Here are the item IDs, found at a GameFAQs forum discussion https://gamefaqs.gamespot.com/boards/196817-breath-of-fire-iii/69678137
Obviously many of these items are not feasible to posesses at this point in the game.
