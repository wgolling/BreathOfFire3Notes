Experience Cap:
--------------

The most experience you can receive in a single fight is 65,535.
For example, Elder normally gives 6000 exp, so with 3 Ivory Dice he gives 48,000.
However, with 4 Iovry Dice he gives 65,535, not 96,000.
Therefore, try not to waste Ivory Dice by using too many.

Elder: still worth it to use 4 => 65,535
Dragon Lord: gives 15000, so use 2 for 60000 (get the extra 5,535 if you REALLY want)
Chimera: gives 9000, so 2 gives 36000 and 3 already gives 65535.

Use as many Ivory Dice against Chimera as you can, but save 2 for Dragon Lord.
